Training Objective

λ_defect = 0.5

Defect classification

Weighted cross-entropy × 6 heads. Class weights balance grade rarity.

λ_vvci = 1.0

vVCI regression

Huber loss (δ=5). Target: vVCI from 6 visual defects, normalised 0–100.

λ_pci = 0.5

PCI regression

Huber loss. Target: ASTM D6433 PCI derived from ground-truth grades.

Loss = 1.0 × Huber(vVCI) + 0.5 × Σ CE(defects) + 0.5 × Huber(PCI)

Two-Stage Training

Stage 1 — Epochs 0–9

Backbone frozen · 835K trainable

Heads trained from scratch on top of fixed ImageNet features. Fast convergence, avoids destroying pretrained representations.

Stage 2 — Epochs 10–39

Top 3 backbone blocks unfrozen · LR × 0.1

Fine-tunes the high-level feature extractor to pavement-specific patterns while keeping lower blocks stable. Prevents catastrophic forgetting.
